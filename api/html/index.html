better tele-task

Better Tele-Task (BTT) is a project by HPI students who love Tele-Task but are sometimes also annoyed by it.

What we do

Subtitles

BTT adds subtitles to Tele-Task videos. These subtitles are generated using publicly available deep-learning-based automatic speech recognition models. Our browser extension fetches subtitles for the video you're currently watching.

Both subtitle generation and storage take place locally on our own server. We do not use your device's resources or any cloud services.

Player tweaks

BTT also adds some quality-of-life features to the Tele-Task player to make it more convenient to use:

  • Double-clicks on the video player toggle fullscreen
  • You can resize both of the videos (camera and screen recording) to 100% width, effectively allowing a fullscreen view of one of the videos
  • Pressing the dedicated play/pause key or "k" on your keyboard pauses/plays the video
